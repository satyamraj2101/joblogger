About Joblogger

Joblogger is a comprehensive job tracking application designed to help you manage your job search effectively. Keep track of job applications, interview stages, and more. Stay organized and focused on your career goals with Joblogger.

For India by KIITians
Joblogger is proudly developed by a team of passionate developers from KIIT (Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology). Our mission is to empower individuals in India with a tool that simplifies the job search process and enhances their career prospects. With Joblogger, we aim to make job tracking and organization an effortless experience for everyone.

Our Vision

At Joblogger, we envision a future where job seekers across India have a user-friendly and efficient platform to manage their job applications seamlessly. Our goal is to contribute to the professional growth of individuals by providing them with a reliable tool for organizing their job search journey.

How Joblogger Works

Joblogger allows users to add and track job applications, categorize them based on status, and visualize their progress. The intuitive interface and powerful features make it easy for users to stay on top of their job search game.
